Instructions

There are currently three permitted actions.

  • Unlock: The unit is added if it wasn't there already, and moreover the unit is automatically unlocked. The first few petitions of this type are automatically approved; the rest are approved manually.
  • Add for later: The unit is added to the list, but not unlocked. Petitions of this form are approved automatically.
  • Drop: This removes a unit entirely from your list. (Be careful what you wish for!) Note that this does not automatically "replace" the dropped unit.
